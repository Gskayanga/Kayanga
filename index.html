About

GS Kayanga is a forward-thinking educational institution dedicated to nurturing academic excellence, discipline, and holistic development among students. Located in KIGALI City, GASABO District, RUTUNGA Sector, KIGABIRO Cell, KIGABIRO Village.

GS Kayanga offers quality education from Nursery school up to lower secondary with a strong emphasis on both academic and moral values.

Our mission is to:

  • Provide a supportive and inclusive learning environment.
  • Foster creativity, critical thinking, and leadership.
  • Prepare students for success in higher education and beyond.

We are committed to empowering every learner with the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in the 21st century.
